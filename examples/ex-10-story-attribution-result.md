Then a GTM engineer stepped in.

Former sales engineer.

Now the agent:
→ Qualifies every inbound lead
→ Sends instant, contextual replies
→ Routes to sales when it makes sense

Key part for me as a GTM guy:
Lead-to-opportunity conversion stayed flat.

Same conversion.
Fewer touches.

The outcome:
→ 9 SDRs moved to outbound
→ Inbound coverage is fully automated
→ Run cost is $1,000 per year

That is a 99.9% cost reduction on inbound pipeline generation.

The GTM engineer is not killing sales.
The GTM engineer is killing busywork.

Jean shared another number:
Salespeople spend 30-40% of time in front of customers today.

With agents doing research, qualification, and follow-up, that number starts to move toward 70%.
