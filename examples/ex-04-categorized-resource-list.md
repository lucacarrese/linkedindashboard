# Example 04 — Categorized Resource List with Social Proof
**Format:** Problem Hook → Social Proof Story → Numbered Categories + Tools + ↳ Descriptions
**Hook type:** Problem + Promise (cost of bad data → 20 solutions)
**CTA type:** Contribution question with 👇

---

Most companies spend thousands of dollars on low-quality data.

Here are 20 data sources to help you avoid this:

Recently, an enterprise company told me they spend $250K quarterly on data

but the quality wasn't great.

So I put together a list of 20 data sources where you can get high-quality data across different niches:

1️⃣ All-in-One Solutions: Instantly.ai and lemlist
All-in-one platforms that handle multiple data needs in one place.

2️⃣ Local Business Tools: Openmart
Great for finding niche local business data organized by geography.

3️⃣ Data Aggregators: Clay and FullEnrich
Pull data from multiple platforms, allowing you to maximize your existing data sources.

4️⃣ Intent Data: Common Room, Unify, Trigify.io, PhantomBuster, @Vector, Crunchbase
Track signals that help you understand prospect willingness to buy, including website visitors, new funding rounds, job changes, and other first-, second-, and third-party intent data signals.

5️⃣ Scraping Tools: ZenRows, Serper, Bardeen
Scrape any data you want from any website.

6️⃣ Enrichment Platforms: Prospeo.io, LeadMagic, Icypeas
Powerful tools for when you don't have all the data you need.
Enrich your lists to transform incomplete data into complete datasets.

7️⃣ B2B Databases: ZoomInfo, Ocean.io
Great when you need large datasets.
Ocean allows you to find lookalike companies based on your existing customers.

8️⃣ Technology Data: Store Leads
Great for understanding what software your clients are using.

Matching good data with automated outbound

Will most likely increase your success rate
