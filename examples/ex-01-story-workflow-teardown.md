**Format:** Story → Problem → Numbered System → Before/After
**Hook type:** Curiosity (subverted expectation)

We just 'hired' our newest team member. No salary, no benefits, no coffee breaks.

Here's how it's going:

Harry Mason and I meet weekly for 45 minutes.

In every meeting, we discuss the same:

→ Internal process improvements
→ Identifying AI agents for operational efficiency
→ Brainstorming content strategies

But ideas stayed in meeting notes.

We did not have time to implement most of them.

Too much strategic value was wasted.

So we built an AI Virtual Assistant that transformed our meetings into executed workflows:

1️⃣ Meeting Intelligence Layer
Attention transcripts → Claude → instant meeting notes with action items → auto-populated in Notion PMS. Fully automated.

2️⃣ Automation Ideation Engine
Claude spots automation opportunities in discussions and delivers ready-to-implement N8N workflows as JSON.

3️⃣ Development Acceleration
V1 in hours. Tested in 24 hours. Live in one week.

4️⃣ Continuous Optimization Loop
Every workflow self-monitors performance. Claude fixes bottlenecks automatically before they cause problems.

Before: Sporadic workflow implementation, maybe one per month
After: Minimum one new automation deployed weekly

We converted 45 minutes of weekly discussion into a systematic workflow factory.

Ideas no longer died in meeting notes.
They became operational infrastructure within days.

The gap between strategic thinking and operational execution just disappeared.
