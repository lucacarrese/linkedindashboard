---

**What it does:**

Your phone sends a message. Your desktop runs the skill. Results come back to your phone.

Think of it as a walkie-talkie for your agent stack. No API keys exposed. No third-party risk.

---

**Here's how it's actually built — 4 layers:**

Layer 1 — Claude Co-work
The brain. The base AI engine that processes every task locally on your machine.

Layer 2 — Your Skills Library
Lead gen, email, content, research, analytics. Any workflow you've built lives here permanently.

Layer 3 — MCP Servers
Gmail, Chrome, Slack, Google Sheets, your file system. The tools Claude calls to get work done.

Layer 4 — Dispatch
Your phone. Send a message from anywhere → it executes locally. Results come back to you.
