# Self-Improving Copy via Instantly — 2026-03-23

Claude Code reads your best-performing campaigns and writes your next one.

Not from a template you wrote once and forgot about.
From your actual live campaign data — reply rates, open rates, sequences that converted.

Here is how it works.

Claude connects to your Instantly account via API. It pulls campaign analytics, ranks by performance, and extracts the copy from the top-performing sequences.

Those become your live frameworks.

When you launch a new campaign, Claude:
→ Finds the best-performing template for your campaign type
→ Injects your new context — persona, offer, goal, date
→ Generates copy that mirrors what already worked

The longer you run it, the better it gets.
Every campaign you launch adds to the data Claude is learning from.

Most teams write copy from scratch every time.
Or they reuse old templates they vaguely remember working.

This does neither. It finds what worked, learns the pattern, and builds on it automatically.

Before: dig through old campaigns manually or start from a blank doc.
After: Claude pulls your best performers and writes the next one in seconds.
