# Claude Code: 57-Second Campaign — 2026-03-23

We built a complete cold email campaign in 57 seconds.
Company list to 154 leads loaded in Instantly. Zero errors. Zero manual work.

Here is the full system.

1️⃣ **Score the companies**
Drop your company list into Claude Code. It reads your ICP scoring criteria and tiers every company — T1, T2, T3, disqualified — using a Python script on the raw CSV data. No guessing. No hallucination.

2️⃣ **Find the contacts**
Tell it the persona you want. Claude hits the Apollo API, pulls the right people at T1 and T2 companies, and returns a breakdown by seniority and contacts per company.

3️⃣ **Enrich the emails**
144 of 159 contacts had emails from Apollo. Claude then hit LeadMagic and Lima Data for the remaining 15. Got 10 more. Done in seconds.

4️⃣ **Write the copy**
Claude connects to Instantly via API, pulls your best-performing campaigns by reply rate, extracts the frameworks, and generates copy for the new campaign. No starting from scratch.

5️⃣ **Create and load the campaign**
Claude creates the campaign in Instantly, injects the copy, uploads all leads, and configures send settings, scheduling, and delivery optimization. All of it.

Before: company list → score manually → find contacts → enrich → write copy → build campaign → upload leads.
After: company list → one prompt → campaign live in Instantly.

The tools did not change. Apollo, Instantly, LeadMagic.
The layer connecting them did.
