# Bottoms-Up Outreach Strategy — 2026-03-23

Most outbound teams go straight to the VP.
We start with the SDR team. Here is why reply rates are higher.

The logic: by the time you reach the decision-maker, the internal conversation has already started.

Here is the approach:

1. **Target the SDR and AE team first**
Send resources, value, webinar invites, lead magnets. Low-stakes outreach. High open rate. They are not the gatekeeper — they are the internal amplifier.

2. **Move up to managers and team leads**
They are talking to their teams. Your name is coming up in internal conversations. Now you have context to reference when you reach out.

3. **Reach the VP or CRO last**
Two things have happened by now. Their team has seen your name. And you can say: "I've already been working with a few people on your team."

4-5 contacts per company. No more.
Enough to create internal noise. Not enough to burn the account.

After 60 days with no reply, re-engage with different people at the same company. The account stays warm. The conversation keeps moving.

Most outbound skips straight to the signature.
Bottoms-up builds the internal case before you ever get there.
