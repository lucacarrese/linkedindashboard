# Post Examples & Swipe File

High-performing structural templates for Luca's content. Always adapt to real experiences — never post generic takes.

---

## Template 1: The Contrarian Take

```
[Bold claim that contradicts common advice]

[Most people do X.]
[We tried X. It didn't work for us because Y.]

[What we do instead:]
→ [Step/principle 1]
→ [Step/principle 2]  
→ [Step/principle 3]

[Result in specific numbers]

[Comment bait question]

[Partner tag if relevant]
```

**Example angle:** "Everyone says hire SDRs before fixing your ops. We did the opposite."

## Hook Formulas

First line is everything. Use these:

- "We [did counterintuitive thing]. [Result]."
- "Most [role]s get this wrong."
- "I spent [X time] figuring out [thing]. Here's what I learned:"
- "[Number] things I wish I knew before [experience]:"
- "Hot take: [Controversial statement]"
- "The real reason [common outcome] happens:"
- "Nobody talks about [thing] enough."
- "[Specific number] changed everything for us."

---

## Comment-Bait Closers

End every post with one of these (vary it):

- "What would you do differently?"
- "Anyone else seeing this in their business?"
- "Drop your approach below 👇"
- "Curious if this matches what you're seeing."
- "What am I missing here?"
- "Has this worked for your team?"
- "What's your benchmark on this?"
- "Agree or disagree?"
