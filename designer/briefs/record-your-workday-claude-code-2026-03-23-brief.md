# Design Brief — record-your-workday-claude-code-2026-03-23

## Post Content
```
1 habit that will change how you use Claude Code:
Record your workday. All of it.

Not a workflow diagram. Not a list of tools you use.
A real recording of what you actually do, hour by hour.

Then give it to Claude Code and ask: what should I automate?

Here is why this works:

1. You stop guessing what to automate
Most teams automate the obvious stuff first. Claude will find the high-leverage tasks — the manual work you have done so many times you stopped noticing it.

2. The output is specific to your business
Not a generic playbook. A system built around how your team actually operates, the tools you use, and the results you are already getting.

3. Claude already has your context
Your ICP, your copy, your tools, your process. Feed it once. It builds on it forever.

The scariest thing about Claude Code is the terminal. It looks like a developer tool.
Once you open it, you realize it is just a chat interface — the same as asking Claude or ChatGPT a question.

The only difference is that this one can actually do things.

Start there. Record a day. See what comes back.

What is one task in your GTM workflow you do manually every single day? 👇
```
